Lihe Zhang

Lihe Zhang is a professor in the School of Information and Communication Engineering at Dalian University of Technology, Dalian, China. He works on deep learning and computer vision. He received the B.S. degree in Optoelectronic Technology in 1999, the M.S. degree in Signal and Information Processing in 2001 with advisor Prof. Chunhui Zhao, from Harbin Engineering University, Harbin, China. He received the Ph.D. degree in Signal and Information Processing in 2004 with advisor Prof. Zhengming Hu and Prof. Yixian Yang from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Beijing, China.
